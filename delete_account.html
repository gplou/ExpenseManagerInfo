Account Deletion — ExpenseManagerAI

You can request the complete deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email to gpmarco@gmail.com with the subject: "Account Deletion Request".
  2. Include the email address associated with your ExpenseManagerAI account.
  3. You will receive a confirmation within 7 business days.
  4. Your account and all your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of confirmation.
Send deletion request

Data that will be deleted

  • User account and login credentials
  • All recorded expenses and income
  • App settings and preferences
  • Activity history associated with your account

Additional retention periods: Due to legal and accounting obligations, certain transaction records may be retained for up to 5 years in anonymized form, with no possibility of linking them to your identity.
